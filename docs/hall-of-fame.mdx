Extension.js and `extension.js.org` are both open-source efforts shaped by contributors across the framework, the docs site, and the surrounding developer experience.

Browser-extension tooling improves because people contribute in different ways: compiler/runtime work, documentation, examples, design, and feedback loops. This page recognizes both sides explicitly.
